What services will you offer?

Pick all that apply. You can run any combination through a single Qualiphy account.

Good Faith Exam (GFE) Most common
A licensed provider clears the patient for an in-clinic procedure. Common for Botox, fillers, IV therapy, and other aesthetic services. No treatment is dispensed.
Rx Treatment
Provider evaluates and, if appropriate, issues a treatment order. Common for GLP-1 weight loss, hormone therapy, and ongoing treatments. Medication ships to the patient or to a pharmacy.
Urgent Care
Same-day consults for UTI, pink eye, cold sores, acne, and similar acute conditions. Treatments are sent to a retail pharmacy of the patient's choice.

How should treatments be fulfilled?

For Rx and Urgent Care exams, choose how the patient gets their medication. Tick all that apply — you can run both flows from the same Qualiphy account.

Qualiphy partner pharmacy Recommended for Rx programs
Pre-vetted compounding pharmacies, bundled all-inclusive pricing. Qualiphy ships directly to the patient. Ideal for GLP-1, peptides, hormone therapy.
Custom pharmacy (CVS, Walgreens, etc.) Required for Urgent Care
Send the treatment order to a specific retail or compounding pharmacy. Patient picks up or has it shipped. Best when patients have a preferred local pharmacy.

Should the provider be able to override your pharmacy or dosage choices during the consultation?

If yes, the licensed provider can pick a different pharmacy or treatment package on the fly — useful when patient needs vary. Otherwise the consultation follows the selections you ship in the payload.

No — follow my settings Default
The provider works within the pharmacy and order set you preselect. No provider_pos_selection in the payload.
Yes — let the provider decide
The provider can swap pharmacy or dosage during the consultation. Sets provider_pos_selection: 3.

Any of these sound useful?

All optional. Most teams ship the basics first and add these later.

Pre-fill patient screening answers from our intake form
If you already collect health info on your site, pass it through so patients don't answer twice. Uses /exam_questions + /exam_submit_answers.
Upload patient documents to Qualiphy automatically
Send lab results, ID photos, or prior records via the API so the provider has them during the screening. Uses /exam_upload_attachment.
Redirect patients back to our site after the screening
Send patients to your own confirmation page (or a different one based on outcome). Uses the redirect_* fields on /exam_invite.
Round-trip our internal order/customer ID through the webhook
Stash a stringified JSON blob in additional_data at invite time and Qualiphy returns it unchanged on every webhook event. Saves a DB lookup when matching results back to your records.
Skip the Qualiphy pharmacy-confirmation screen for custom pharmacy invites
By default, Qualiphy asks the patient to confirm their pharmacy on our end. Pick this if your platform already chose the custom pharmacy (or your own UI asks the patient) so they aren't asked again. We'll show you how to look up the right pharmacy via /partner_pharmacy_list + /custom_pharmacy_search and pre-populate every field on /exam_invite.
Bill the clinic for custom-pharmacy treatments (instead of the patient)
Sets custom_pharmacy_patient_billing: 1 and adds a custom_pharmacy_clinic_billing identifier you'll fill in. Off by default — patient is billed by the pharmacy.
Custom pharmacy ships to the patient (instead of in-pharmacy pickup)
Sets custom_pharmacy_delivery_method: 2. Off by default — patient picks up at the pharmacy.
Skip the Qualiphy patient-address confirmation screen for medication exams
By default, patients confirm their home (and shipping) address on Qualiphy's screening form. Pick this if you already collect those addresses on your end and want to bypass the screen — we'll add the relevant address_* (and, for partner-pharmacy Rx, shipping_address_*) fields to your /exam_invite payload sample.
